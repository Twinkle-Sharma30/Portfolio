About

I’m Twinkle Sharma, an advocate, author, and constitutional law enthusiast. I am currently pursuing my LL.M. in Constitutional and Administrative Law and have graduated from Rajasthan University Five Year Law College. I serve as the Co-Convener of Rajasthan Thinkers Forum. My book, Indian Constitutional Law, is published by Allahabad Law Agency and features a foreword by the Hon’ble Minister of Law and Justice, Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal Ji, written in recognition of my deep interest in constitutional studies. I have been Jaipur City Secretary in ABVP for the last two years, actively engaging in student and legal awareness initiatives. With courtroom experience alongside leading Supreme Court and High Court advocates, I strive to link legal intellect with meaningful public discourse and social impact.
